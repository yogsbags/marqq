# Tara — Marketing Audit & Scoring

## When to Use
When the user asks for a marketing audit, wants a score for a company's marketing, or needs a comprehensive assessment before building strategy.

## Marketing Audit Score (0-100)

Evaluate and score across 6 weighted dimensions:

| Dimension | Weight | Focus |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Content & Messaging | 25% | Value prop clarity, headline quality, social proof, CTA |
| Conversion Optimization | 20% | Signup friction, landing page quality, trust at decision points |
| SEO & Discoverability | 20% | Title tags, meta, heading structure, page speed signals |
| Competitive Positioning | 15% | Differentiation, market category clarity |
| Brand & Trust | 10% | Visual consistency, security signals, team transparency |
| Growth & Strategy | 10% | Content depth, email capture, retargeting, upsell |

### Composite Score Formula:
```
Score = Content(0.25) + Conversion(0.20) + SEO(0.20) + Competitive(0.15) + Brand(0.10) + Growth(0.10)
```

### Grade Scale:
- 85-100 → A (Excellent)
- 70-84 → B (Good)
- 55-69 → C (Average)
- 40-54 → D (Below average)
- 0-39 → F (Critical)

## Scoring Rubric (per dimension)

**85-100**: Best-in-class execution, hard to find faults
**70-84**: Solid fundamentals, minor gaps
**55-69**: Functional but misses key opportunities
**40-54**: Significant gaps impeding performance
**0-39**: Major problems requiring immediate attention

## Recommendation Tiers

**Quick Wins (this week)**: Copy changes, meta descriptions, trust signals, CTA improvements
**Strategic (this month)**: Pricing page, comparison pages, lead magnets
**Long-Term (this quarter)**: Content overhaul, SEO campaign, funnel redesign

## Output Format for Audit Tasks

Always use this structure for marketing audit task types:

```

### Score Breakdown
| Dimension | Score | Key Finding |
|-----------|-------|-------------|

| Conversion | XX/100 | [Finding] |
| SEO | XX/100 | [Finding] |

```

## India/B2B Benchmarks
- Good SaaS India landing page converts at 3-5% (visitor → trial)
- Trust signals especially important: team page, GST reg, Indian payment methods
- WhatsApp CTA often outperforms email CTA for India-first products
- Hero headline test is the single highest-impact change (typically +20-40% lift)
