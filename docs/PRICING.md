## Value Metric

**Primary: Workspaces (companies)**
Each workspace = one company's MKG. Clean scaling unit — an agency with 5 clients pays for 5 workspaces. Customers naturally expand as they grow.

**Secondary (future): AI Credits** for heavy agent run volume above tier limits. Hold off until post-launch — complexity kills early conversion.

## Rationale

### India ₹12,999/month Growth
- Replaces a ₹50K agency → Marqq is 26% of that cost
- Equivalent to one junior contractor day — easy to justify
- Annual deal ₹1.25L fits in a typical SMB software budget without board approval

### Global $249/month Growth
- HubSpot Pro alone is $890/month → Marqq is less than 1/3 of that
- Semrush Guru ($249) + any one other tool = more spend for less capability
- Annual $2,390 clears most SMB software budgets without CFO sign-off

## Tier Packaging Logic

**Starter** — Trial funnel. Gates 6 of 12 agents and 100 runs/month. Buyers hit the ceiling fast and upgrade. Don't launch this until Phase 2.

**Growth (Recommended)** — Target 70%+ of customers here. All 12 agents, unlimited runs, all 9 modules. 2.6x Starter price but 5x+ value.

**Scale** — Multi-brand companies, holding companies, consultants managing 2–3 clients. 3-workspace bundle.

**Agency/Enterprise** — Sales-led. Custom deal. 10+ workspaces, white-label, SLA.

---

## Pricing Psychology

1. **Lead with Growth as "Recommended"** — Decoy effect: Starter looks limited, Scale looks like overkill, Growth looks obvious.
2. **Annual-first UI** — Show annual by default. Monthly toggle exists but is secondary. Lifts LTV immediately.
3. **INR for India, USD for global** — Never show USD to Indian buyers. ₹12,999 reads as ₹13K; $156 reads as "foreign software."
4. **Frame against agency, not tools** — "Less than one agency day per month" for India. "Less than HubSpot alone" for global.
5. **14-day free trial of Growth, not freemium** — Freemium at this product complexity creates support cost without conversion. Trial creates urgency.
